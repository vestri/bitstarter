Keeping schedule is challenging for everybody, mainly for kids. It is difficult to perceive the time passing while practicing activities. Kid scheduler is a tool that will guide kids in accomplishing daily activities such as the morning activity, where a list of tasks (waking up, dressing...) should be achieved on time in order to be on time for the school. This application can be implemented later on different hardware: a smartphone, an alarm clock, a watch, a tablet, or a toy such as a teddy bear.

Kid Scheduler is an application that will help them to keep their schedule by having a good real-time time perception in a positive manner. They receive a recompence when their are succeeded. In addition, it reduces parent/kids conflicts by transfering parent's orders to the application. Parents have a new relation with kids by helping them to succeed in accomplishing the tasks.
